What is SAF?

Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) is produced from renewable feedstocks — primarily waste cooking oil (UCO) — rather than fossil fuels. Through HEFA refining, UCO is converted into a jet fuel molecularly identical to conventional kerosene, capable of reducing lifecycle CO₂ emissions by up to 85%. IATA projects SAF will contribute 65% of aviation's carbon reduction by 2050.

02 · 生物燃料企业出海认证咨询

The licence
to sell green
globally.

Chinese biofuel feedstocks and finished SAF cannot enter international markets without passing sustainability certification — primarily RSB (Roundtable on Sustainable Biomaterials) and ISCC (International Sustainability and Carbon Certification). These schemes verify feedstock origin, carbon accounting, and supply chain traceability.

As part of MotionECO's commercial team, I supported five Chinese biofuel firms through the certification process — coordinating documentation, audit preparation, and compliance review. The cohort included Zhenhai Refinery (SAF producer) and Junshang Environmental (UCO feedstock supplier) as lead cases, alongside three additional firms.

SAF技术路线 · Five Production Pathways

HEFA

酯类和脂肪酸类加氢工艺

通过加氢工艺提炼废弃油脂生产SAF，转化率35–40%。技术最成熟，Honeywell UOP、Neste NExBTL均属此类。中国在产项目主流路线。

转化率 35–40%

G+FT

费托合成工艺

以合成气为原料经费托反应生成液态烃。化工领域应用成熟，但SAF转化率仅10–15%，碱金属对催化剂有害，生产成本较高。

转化率 10–15%

AtJ

醇喷合成工艺

将醇类化合物转化为碳氢化合物，原料多为糖类或农林废弃物。LanzaTech、Gevo采用该技术，适合农林资源丰富的国家，中国成本较高。

转化率 ~60%

PtL

电转液工艺

利用可再生能源电解水制氢，结合碳捕集技术合成液态碳氢化合物。理论产能无上限，随绿电供应量提升成本降低，欧洲多项目已进入前期工程阶段。

长期最优路线

CHJ

催化热水解工艺

通过催化水解将脂肪酸酯和游离脂肪酸转化为SAF，经水处理、加氢裂化、分馏等步骤精制。仍处于小规模测试阶段，商业化尚早。
