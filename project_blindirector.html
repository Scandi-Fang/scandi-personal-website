01 · The Challenge

17.3 million
people. Zero
adequate tools.

As of 2016, China's visually impaired population stands at approximately 17.3 million — 1.26% of the total population. The assistive technology available to this group is either prohibitively expensive, unavailable in the Chinese market, or fails to deliver the directional precision needed for independent urban navigation.

Core user needs

01

Live individually and conveniently — without constant reliance on human assistance.

02

A reliable sense of security — confident awareness of the immediate environment.

03

Directive feedback — precise, real-time instruction rather than passive obstacle alerts.

02 · The Benchmarking

Every existing
solution misses
the mark.

Three incumbent solutions were evaluated across the three dimensions that matter most to Chinese visually impaired users: cost accessibility, domestic market availability, and navigational precision. None cleared all three criteria — leaving a clear gap that BlinDirector was designed to close.

Biped AI

Wearable technology with in-built 3D cameras to warn users about the position of obstacles around them.

Advantages

  • ·Directive and sensitive (w/GPS)
  • ·Long view range
  • ·Danger detection
  • ·Long battery life

Disadvantages

  • ·Expensive — ¥31,605 RMB total
  • ·Only available in EU & US
  • ·Hard to operate
Guide Dog · CN Market

A trained guide dog allows visually impaired people to navigate independently, providing multi-faceted support.

Advantages

  • ·Strong feedback and support
  • ·High commitment
  • ·Help in various ways

Disadvantages

  • ·Hard to acquire
  • ·Few percentage of dogs trained
WeWalk Smart Cane

A smart cane that integrates digital technology with the support of a companion app for enhanced navigation.

Advantages

  • ·Convenient and phone-connected
  • ·Automatic voice feedback

Disadvantages

  • ·Expensive — ¥2,443 RMB total
  • ·Limited directive feedback

03 · The Tech

Built from
the ground up.

Vision · OpenMV 4Plus

Image
Intelligence

The OpenMV 4Plus module delivers efficient real-time image recognition — including traffic-light state detection — and manages all upper/lower computer information transmission via a low-latency serial protocol.

Mobility · Mecanum Wheels

Omni-
directional

Customised Mecanum wheel configuration enables full omnidirectional movement — lateral, diagonal, and rotational — without requiring the user to reorient the device. Critical for dense urban pedestrian environments.

Architecture · Modular Design

Field-
repairable

A fully modular internal architecture allows individual subsystems to be swapped out for repair or upgraded independently — minimising downtime and reducing long-term service costs without specialised tooling.
